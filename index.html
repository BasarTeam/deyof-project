What is the true meaning of coffee?

Coffee, a beloved elixir cherished by millions around the world, has the power to transform ordinary moments into extraordinary ones. It serves as a faithful companion during quiet mornings, a source of inspiration during late-night work sessions, and a catalyst for meaningful conversations with friends and loved ones. From its humble origins to its global presence today, coffee has woven itself into the fabric of human culture, transcending borders and connecting people from all walks of life.
